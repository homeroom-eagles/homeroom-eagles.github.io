Homeroom Eagles

Beyond Academics.

A classroom with a homely atmosphere

Like the eagle, we instill values like courage and strength.

Timings

We function from 16:30 to 19:30 on weekdays, 15:00 to 18:00 on Saturdays and Sundays are holidays.

Emphasis on Communication Skills

Through communication, we strive to bridge the gap between classroom education and a real world scenario.

Our Curriculum and Goals

Curriculum

Sessions are designed for age groups 3-5 (Level 1) and above 5 years of age (Level 2). To improve self-confidence and speaking skills, exclusive sessions on "Speaking on any/given topic" and "Quiz Programs" are periodically conducted.

Goals

The sessions are designed to help students achieve self-awareness, understanding, belief and acceptance. We aim at the formation of values, attitudes and leadership skills in children.

Future Endeavors

  • As part of our vision for the future, we plan to improve the skills imparted to the students by adding more activities.
  • We are also planning to include a foreign and national language programme to the existing curriculum.
